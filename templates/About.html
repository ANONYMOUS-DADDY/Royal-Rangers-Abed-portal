About Royal Rangers Abakaliki East District

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to evangelize, equip, and empower the next generation of Christlike men and lifelong servant leaders. Our vision is to see every boy and young man grow into a mature and responsible adult, equipped with the skills and values needed to succeed in life.

Our History

Royal Rangers Abakaliki East District began with a passion for youth development. Established in the early 2000s, our district has been dedicated to providing mentorship and leadership opportunities for young people in Ebonyi State, Nigeria. Through our programs, we have helped countless individuals grow spiritually, socially, and mentally.

Leadership

Our leadership team is comprised of dedicated individuals who are committed to guiding young lives. The District Commanding Officer (DCO), along with other leadership members, work to ensure that all programs align with our mission and vision. Together, we provide a supportive and nurturing environment for youth to develop their leadership potential.
