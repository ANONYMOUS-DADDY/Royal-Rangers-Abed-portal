Welcome to Royal Rangers Abakaliki East District

About Us

The Royal Rangers is a mentoring program for future men and women with a mission to evangelize, equip, and empower the next generation of Christlike leaders. Our district is committed to providing young people with the tools they need to succeed spiritually, physically, mentally, and socially.

Programs

  • Leadership Training Modules
  • Outdoor Adventure Activities
  • Discipleship and Bible Study Programs
  • Community Service Initiatives

Units

Discovery Rangers

For boys and girls aged 6–8, focusing on basic skills and spiritual growth.

Adventure Rangers

For boys and girls aged 9–12, emphasizing leadership and teamwork.

Expedition Rangers

For teens aged 13–17, preparing for adulthood through advanced skills training.

Leaders Unit

A group of dedicated mentors providing guidance and support.
