Welcome Message

Welcome to the Royal Rangers Abakaliki East District portal. Our mission is to mentor and guide young people in their spiritual, physical, and social development through engaging programs and activities.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to evangelize, equip, and empower the next generation of Christlike men and lifelong servant leaders. Our vision is to see every boy and young man grow into a mature and responsible adult, equipped with the skills and values needed to succeed in life.

Programs Overview

Training Modules
