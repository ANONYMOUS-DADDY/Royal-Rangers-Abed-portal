Our Mission

Our mission is to evangelize, equip, and empower the next generation of Christ-like servant leaders. We aim to provide a nurturing environment where young people can grow in their faith and develop essential life skills.

Our Vision

Our vision is to see young people transformed into Christ-like servant leaders. We strive to create opportunities for personal growth, community service, and leadership development through various programs and activities.

Our Values

Our core values are evangelism, discipleship, leadership, achievement, and service. We believe in fostering a supportive community where young people can thrive and make a positive impact in their world.

We offer a variety of programs and activities designed to help young people grow in their faith, develop leadership skills, and serve their communities. From weekly meetings to special events and service projects, there is something for everyone at Royal Rangers Abakaliki East District.
